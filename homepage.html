ABOUT US

Grove Collaborative is an online retailer specializing in eco-friendly, sustainable household and personal care products. The company focuses on delivering high-quality, non-toxic, and environmentally responsible goods, ranging from cleaning supplies to skincare, aimed at reducing waste and promoting healthier lifestyles. Key features include: Plastic-Free Initiatives : Committed to becoming 100% plastic-free by 2025. Subscription Model: Offers a flexible auto-ship subscription for convenience. Certified B Corporation : Prioritizes social and environmental impact alongside profitability. Exclusive Products: Provides its own branded, sustainable products alongside other trusted eco-friendly brands. Their mission is to make sustainable living accessible to everyone while driving positive environmental change.
